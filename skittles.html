The skittles project

The skittles project was a project for my CS class.
In the project, we collected data to answer an intersting question about skittles,
In our group, our qestion was how the flavor of the skittle affects its density,
We measured the skittles' mass and volume to find their densities.
We found that the color of the skittle didn't make too much of a difference.
